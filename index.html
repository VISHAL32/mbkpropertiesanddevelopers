F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is TS RERA?

    The TSRERA maintains a record of recognized real estate projects accessible to everyone on its main site. Additionally, the TSRERA requires that every real estate project advertisement include the registration number.

  • What is HMDA Approved mean?

    The Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority (HMDA) was formed by an Act (G.O.Ms.No.570 MA & UD (11) Dept., dt.25.08.2008) of the then combined state of Andhra Pradesh Government in the year 2008, with an area of 7,257 Sq Km under its purview.

  • What is DTCP?

    The Directorate of Town and Country Planning (DTCP) is a government body responsible for the planning and development of towns and cities

  • Why to invest on Lands/Plots?

    Land exits before and will be later. Real estate /Lands/Plots invest is future proof, recession proof, which means the value can never be depriciated.

  • Why not GP Layouts? Why only RERA, HMDA, DTCP

    GP Layouts are unapproved layout done by private persons. Whereas HMDA, DTCP and RERA are private but approved only when they meet the government rules and regulations.
